## THE ONE-LINE PITCH

RingPaw is an AI receptionist built for dog groomers — it answers calls, books appointments into your calendar, and texts confirmations automatically, so you never lose a booking because your hands were full.

---

## WHO YOU'RE TALKING TO

**Primary target:** Solo groomers or 1–3 person shops. They're doing every groom themselves. The phone rings mid-bath, mid-haircut, mid-nail trim. They can't answer. The customer doesn't leave a voicemail. They call the next groomer on Google.

**Secondary target:** Established salons with front-desk gaps, part-time staff, or after-hours volume.

**Psychographic:** Owners who are great at grooming but exhausted by the business side. They're not looking for software — they want their phone problem solved.

## OBJECTION HANDLING

### "I'm not sure my customers will want to talk to an AI."
> Totally fair concern — and honestly the most common one. But when we let groomers listen back to calls, the #1 reaction is "I can't believe that's AI." It sounds natural, asks the right questions, and handles the booking faster than most humans. The customer just wants their dog booked — they don't care who (or what) does it. We have a live demo number — want to call it right now and hear for yourself?

### "I already have voicemail."
> Most customers don't leave voicemails. They hang up and call the next groomer. Voicemail is a holding pen — RingPaw is a booking machine. The difference is whether that caller becomes your client or your competitor's.

### "It's too expensive."
> At $99 a month, one extra booking covers it. Most groomers tell us they were missing 2–4 calls a day — at $85 a groom, that's $170–$340 in lost revenue every single day. RingPaw pays for itself the first morning it's live. And if it doesn't book anything in 30 days, you don't pay — that's our guarantee.

### "I'm too busy to set this up."
> Setup takes 5 minutes, start to finish. You enter your business name, your services, and link your calendar. We walk you through setting up call forwarding — it's a single code you dial on your phone, takes 30 seconds. That's it. Nothing to maintain after that.

### "What if it gets something wrong?"
> It only books into slots that are genuinely open in your calendar — it can't double-book. If it's unsure about something, it says so and asks the customer to clarify rather than guessing. You can also text it simple commands to block days, adjust hours, or pause bookings anytime. And you get a daily report showing every call it handled.

### "I need to think about it / talk to my partner."
> Totally — and that makes sense for bigger decisions. But given the 30-day guarantee, there's no risk to trying it. Worst case you cancel before day 30 and pay nothing. Want to get it set up today so you can actually see it working with real calls this week, rather than wondering?

### "I use Square / Google / Acuity — will it work?"
> Yes — RingPaw connects directly to all three. It reads your live availability from your calendar and books straight into it. No manual syncing, no double-entry.

### "I already have a front desk person."
> Great — RingPaw is a great backup for when they're unavailable: before hours, after hours, lunch breaks, or when they're already on a call. A lot of salons use it to make sure zero calls slip through, even with a full-time person on staff.

### "What if I want to cancel?"
> Month-to-month, no cancellation fee, no penalty. You can cancel anytime. We'd rather earn your business every month than lock you in.
